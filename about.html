About Game Infinity

" Game Infinity " was founded in 2019 by Avdhut Palande along with Gunvant Mokal and Amey Patil with the goal of bringing great freeware and shareware games to the entire world.

Avdhut, Gunvant and Amey accomplished his vision as Game Infinity has been visited and enjoyed by millions of gamers over the years.

In March of 2019, Code Infinity, Inc. purchased Game Infinity with the commitment to carry on Avdhut’s vision of organizing and categorizing the best computer games available across all platforms.

Code Infinity will look to expand on this goal as well by establishing a thriving and enthusiastic game community by offering iWin’s unique and original game content.
